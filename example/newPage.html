f a Q

1. What is the recommended age limit?

here is no age limit, but we do require adult supervision for children below 13. Children aged 7 or below can enter for free with adult supervision.

For example, if you have 7 players and two of them are children below 7 years old, you may proceed to book for 5 pax while the 2 pax children can play for free! However, we do not recommend age below 7 years old to participate in the game as it may be too complicated for them and is not designed for this age group.

2. Is the game suitable for the weak-hearted, pregnant ladies or those with disabilities?

Some of our games might have some surprise elements and minimal physical obstacles such as climbing or crawling. However, if they are not keen on continuing the game, our game masters will definitely guide them out of the room immediately as requested.

3. How long do escape rooms take?

How long it takes you to escape from the room is entirely down to your team. Each experience usually lasts between 45 and 110 minutes. This consists of a 10-15minute brief, up to 90 minutes of game play time within the room itself and a short 5 minute debrief and photo opportunity at the end.

4. How many people should you do an escape room with?

In order for those parts of an escape room to be utilized, you can refer to the “Theme” section regarding the number of players required in each story. The middle ground will allow you to spread yourselves out equally, communicate without too much distraction, and focus on the task at hand.

5. When do I need to arrive?

You don’t want to show up late for a game that’s all about beating the clock. All “escapers” should arrive at least 15 minutes before the scheduled game.

6. What type of attire is suitable?

Comfortable clothing and shoes that are easy to move around are recommended because there will be slight physical obstacles in the games.

7. Are phones allowed in escape rooms?

Phones are not allowed in our escape rooms. We have a safety locker for you to keep all your belongings.

8. Do I have to make a booking online or can I walk-in?

Both methods would do! However, we would advise online booking to secure your game session, especially during the weekends. You can now book a room and make payment online before heading to our outlet.

9. What should I do if I received an ‘Unsuccessful’ message after the online booking payment?

Check if your card / account was charged. If no, please proceed to repurchase the tickets. If yes, please check your transaction history page or check your email for the order confirmation.

10. What should I do if I realized that I purchased the wrong ticket(s) after payment process has completed?

All internet purchases are confirmed purchases and any request for refunds, exchanges or cancellations will not be entertained.
